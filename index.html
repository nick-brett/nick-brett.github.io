About

Creative, Driven, and Independent.

Where most would thrive in a well scheduled environment, I feel most at home when left to my own devices. From my years of sales experience I've learned that I like being prepared, but I thrive when it comes to improvising.

After working sales in the home remodeling industry for a couple of years while attending college, I discovered my latent passion for web development.

From there I attended Lambda School and was given the opportunity to put my theoretical knowledge of computer science into practical application.

I am based in the DMV area looking for work as a full-stack web developer.
Preferred positions would be in one of the major hubs such as DC, NYC, or LA.

In case I look familiar, I also happen to have a twin brother–– as seen above.
